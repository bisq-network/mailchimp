Challenging the community

Milestone v0.1 is achieved and we need to fund future work!

Just after Coinbase raised $75 million, we hope the community can show that it’s possible to kick off the creation of a fully decentralized bitcoin exchange with 120 BTC (25 000 USD). Manfred wrote an important blog post to show why centralized exchanges are dangerous to Bitcoin.

 

Now it’s time to find a sustainable way to fund the development of our next milestones.

 

Decentralized Funding

Using Lighthouse to raise BTC

We believe our vision of a fully decentralized Bitcoin exchange is shared by many. If you want to support our efforts, please tell your friends about Bitsquare. Your support and encouragement keeps us going!

The Lighthouse decentralized peer-to-peer crowdfunding application is ideally suited for funding decentralized bitcoin projects that serve as a common good. Lighthouse is a desktop application that allows pledges to be made without a middle man.

To submit your pledge in bitcoin you must download a desktop application from the Lighthouse page. Only after the goal is reached, pledges will be paid. If we do not reach our funding goal then you can revoke your pledge.

This innovative new funding model gives users maximum control and 100% of the money you pledge goes to the project; no middleman takes a cut or is in control of the bitcoins you pledge.

 

To learn more about our crowdfunding, please visit our website. You can find all the details there.

 

Check out our blog post discussing more in depth the reasons why we have chosen an iterative crowd funding model.
