Bitsquare Beta launch

The official Beta version is out

Our team is proud to announce that Bitsquare is now officially in Beta! After 3 months of extensive testing on the Bitcoin mainnet, The Decentralized Bitcoin Exchange is now ripe for a wider audience.

 

Here is what you can do:

- Download the official Beta release and start trading.

- Contribute your skills and help improve Bitsquare.

- Join the community in our Forum, IRC and Slack.

- Follow Bitsquare on Twitter, Github and on our Blog.

- Attend in person one of our upcoming Beta launch events in Europe.


We take the opportunity to remind you that unlike traditional centralized exchanges, Bitsquare requires no registration or verification process and does not hold user’s data or funds. Communication is end-to-end encrypted and routed over Tor. Trades use three levels of protection: security deposit, 2-of-3 multisignature escrow address, and a decentralized arbitration system.

We are looking forward to see you on the network!
The Bitsquarre team
