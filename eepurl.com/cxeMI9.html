Bitsquare is rebranding

Bounty of 0.5 BTC for proposing the winning name

 

Recently our team initiated registration of the Bitsquare brand. Unfortunately the name was opposed with little chance to successfully register it. To avoid a lengthy and expensive legal process, we will have to find another name.  

 

Bitsquare is offering a bounty of 0.5 bitcoin for the person whose proposed name ends up being selected by the team. The details of the bounty are available on the official Bitsquare forum.

 

Here is what you can do next:

  • Read our blog post announcement on the rebranding.

  • Think of a cool new name that resonates with Bitsquare’s principles and philosophy.

  • Post your proposals on the forum.

  • Discuss other name proposals by the community.


Best regards,

The Bitsquare team
